Parental Outcomes of Gay Father Families in China

Over the last 30 years, the discussion of same sex marriage is evolving massively forward. A lot of academics has agreed on the fact that children’s development outcome and will not significantly differ from those coming from heterosexual families. (Golombok & Tasker, 1996,
Anderssen et al., 2002, Golombok et al., 2003, Croch et al., 2014) The nuisances outside the closet lingered through decades. It involves hardships in getting custody (Bozett, 1987, Bailey et al., 1995, Stacey & Biblarz, 2001), opposition against same-sex parenthood (Warde, 1997,
Lerner & Nagai, 2000), and institutionalized discrimination against LGBTQs. (Stacey & Biblarz, 2001) Despite the progress being made, some major problems remains. One being the imbalancement between gender, another being insufficiency of cultural variation.
